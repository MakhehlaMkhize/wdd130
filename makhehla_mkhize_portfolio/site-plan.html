Overview

Purpose

How would it make you feel if you could have a website idea overnight, and in the morning, you wake up with confidence that you have someone under your wing that is ready to make that idea a reality without much supervision? Well, here is an opportunity to employ one of the most detail-oriented, self-driven, and creative individuals, that is ready to share his knowledge and passion within the structure of your company. My portfolio is evidence of the endless possibilities that can be created right before your eyes.

Audience

The intended audience is potential employers that are looking for a front-end developer to add to their team. It is also for any business or company looking for a freelance front-end developer.
